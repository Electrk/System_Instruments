If you want a phrase to repeat forever, add a repeat <font:Arial Bold:18>% <font:Arial:16>symbol at the end.

Example:
<lmargin%:10>
<font:Arial Bold:20>C4, D4, E4, F4, G4, A4, B4, C5, <color:FF0000>%<color:000000>
<lmargin%:8><font:Arial:16>

Please note that anything after the <font:Arial Bold:18>% <font:Arial:16>symbol will <font:Arial Bold:16>NOT <font:Arial:16>be played.
