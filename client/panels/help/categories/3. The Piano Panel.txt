The piano panel is where phrase creation takes place.  A <font:Arial Bold:16>phrase<font:Arial:16> is a short piece of music that is no longer than CONST_MAX_PHRASE_LENGTH characters.

Phrases can be put together to form a <font:Arial Bold:16>song<font:Arial:16> but we'll get to that later.
