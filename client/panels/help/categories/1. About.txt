<font:Arial Bold:16>Playable Instruments <font:Arial:16>is an add-on that lets you play musical instruments in-game, either via premade songs/phrases, or live using your keyboard.

It also adds a modular system for loading add-ons with the <font:Arial Bold:16>Instrument_<font:Arial:16> prefix.
